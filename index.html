Chocolate Cake Recipe

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add butter, eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving.
